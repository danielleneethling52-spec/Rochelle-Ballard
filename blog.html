Why Knowledge is Power in Manufacturing

Navigating the world of international trade requires more than just a good product idea; it requires an intimate understanding of Quality Assurance (QA) and Quality Control (QC). Many first-time importers confuse the two, but at RBA Inspections, we believe our clients should be as informed as our inspectors. QA is about the process—ensuring the factory has the right systems. QC is about the product—ensuring the individual unit meets your spec.

Our blog serves as a resource for Supply Chain Managers, CEOs, and Procurement Officers who want to minimize their "Total Cost of Quality." From explaining the intricacies of ISO 9001 certification to providing checklists for Container Loading Supervision, our goal is to empower your business. A single mistake in a production run can set a brand back by years; staying educated through our weekly insights is your first line of defense against manufacturing failure.
