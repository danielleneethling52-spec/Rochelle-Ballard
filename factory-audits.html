Know Who You Are Buying From

The global marketplace is full of "trading companies" posing as manufacturers. While they might provide a good price, they lack the direct control over quality that a factory owner possesses. RBA’s Factory Audit services identify the truth behind the website.

Our Supplier Evaluation is designed to protect you from the "Sample Bait and Switch." We confirm that the factory shown in the photos is actually where your goods will be produced, and that they have the machinery and labor force required to hit your deadlines.

The Audit Methodology

An RBA auditor spends a full day on-site, performing a 360-degree review of the facility. We don't just sit in the manager's office; we walk the floor, interview staff, and check the calibration dates on the testing equipment.

Our reports provide a "weighted score" across different departments, giving you a clear "Pass," "Fail," or "Corrective Action Needed" result. This data is vital for procurement teams who need to justify their supplier selection to stakeholders.

Ethical Sourcing & Social Compliance

Modern consumers care about where their products come from. An unethical supplier can destroy a brand overnight. Our audits include a deep dive into the facility's social responsibility, checking for fair wages, safe working conditions, and environmental management. By choosing RBA, you ensure your supply chain remains transparent and ethical.
