Standardizing Quality in a Global Market

In today's complex supply chain environment, the distance between a brand and its manufacturer can lead to significant quality drift. RBA Inspections acts as your onsite technical advocate. We don't just "check boxes"; we analyze the structural integrity, aesthetic consistency, and functional reliability of your products using internationally recognized ISO 2859-1 (AQL) sampling plans.

Our methodology is rooted in transparency. When you engage RBA, you aren't just getting a PDF report—you're getting a real-time window into the factory floor. We serve industries ranging from consumer electronics and heavy machinery to luxury textiles and automotive components.

Product Inspections (DPI & FRI)

Whether it is During Production (DPI) or a Final Random Inspection (FRI), our engineers meticulously verify that your batch meets 100% of your specified criteria. We focus on identifying "Critical," "Major," and "Minor" defects before the goods leave the facility.

  • Workmanship: Visual audit for scratches, burrs, or assembly errors.
  • On-site Testing: Rub tests, drop tests, hi-pot tests, and function cycles.
  • Packaging: Verification of barcodes, shipping marks, and drop-test compliance.

Factory Audits & Supplier Evaluation

A high-quality sample doesn't guarantee a high-quality partner. Our Factory Audits deep-dive into the manufacturer’s DNA. We evaluate their Quality Management System (QMS) based on ISO 9001 standards to ensure they have the infrastructure to sustain long-term production.

"We verify factory licenses, production lines, R&D capabilities, and social compliance to ensure your brand isn't exposed to ethical risks."

  • Verification of production capacity and lead-time reliability.
  • Evaluation of raw material storage and warehouse management.

Pre-Shipment & Loading Supervision

The final stage of the supply chain is often the most vulnerable. Our Container Loading Supervision (CLS) ensures that the correct quantity of goods is loaded into a structurally sound container, and that the palletization prevents transit damage.

We document the entire process, from the arrival of the container to the application of the lead seal, providing you with 100% certainty that what you paid for is what is on the ship.
