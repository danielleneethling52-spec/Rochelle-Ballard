Real-world data on how we protect our clients' bottom line across the globe.

Electronic Component Validation

Challenge: A major European distributor was facing a 15% return rate on IoT devices manufactured in Shenzhen. Traditional factory testing was passing faulty boards.

Solution: RBA implemented a customized functional cycle test during a DPI (During Production Inspection). Our engineers found that high-humidity environments were causing circuit shorts not tested in the factory's dry QC lab.

Result: Defect rates dropped to 0.8% in the next shipment. Savings estimated at $140,000 per quarter.

Textile Quality & Ethical Audit

Challenge: An upscale fashion brand needed to verify that their new organic cotton supplier in India actually adhered to GOTS standards and social compliance (BSCI).

Solution: RBA performed a multi-day Social Systems Audit, verifying labor contracts, safety protocols, and chemical disposal logs alongside fabric pull-tests.

Result: The client avoided a potential PR disaster by identifying an unauthorized subcontractor using non-certified dyes.
