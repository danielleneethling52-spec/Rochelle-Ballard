Real-world data on how we protect our clients' bottom line across the globe.

Electronic Component Validation

Challenge: A major European distributor was facing a 15% return rate on IoT devices manufactured in Shenzhen. Traditional factory testing was passing faulty boards.

Solution: RBA implemented a customized functional cycle test during DPI. Our engineers found that high-humidity environments were causing shorts not caught in dry QC labs.

Result: Defect rates dropped to 0.8%. Estimated savings of $140,000 per quarter.

Textile Quality & Ethical Audit

Challenge: An upscale brand needed to verify organic cotton suppliers in India adhered to GOTS standards and social compliance (BSCI).

Solution: RBA performed a multi-day Social Systems Audit, verifying labor contracts, safety protocols, and chemical logs alongside fabric tests.

Result: The client avoided a PR disaster by identifying an unauthorized subcontractor using non-certified dyes.
